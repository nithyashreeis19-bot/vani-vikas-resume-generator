Part 1: Product Requirements Document (PRD)1. Product Vision & Problem StatementVision: To bridge the digital divide for skilled Indian workers (e.g., electricians, drivers, security guards) by enabling them to create professional, ATS-friendly resumes through simple voice interactions in their native language.Problem: Approximately 75% of resumes are rejected by ATS systems before reaching humans. Furthermore, many skilled laborers possess high technical proficiency ("Hand Skills") but lack the "Digital Literacy" required to navigate complex resume-building software.2. Target AudienceSemi-skilled and skilled laborers (Electricians, Truck Drivers, Security Guards, Housekeeping Staff).Users with limited computer literacy who are comfortable with smartphones and voice messaging.3. Functional RequirementsMultilingual Voice Interface: The system must support voice input and output in 22 official Indian languages (e.g., Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Marathi).Interactive AI Interviewer: Instead of a form, the AI will "interview" the user, asking sequential questions about personal info, skills, and work history.Real-time AI Refinement: Each raw voice response (e.g., "I fix wires in big buildings") must be refined and professionalized by AI (e.g., "Experienced in commercial electrical maintenance and wiring").Pre-defined Resume Formatting: The final output must be a professionally formatted resume (PDF) that is optimized for ATS.Progress Persistence: User data and conversation logs must be stored in a database to allow for session resumption.4. Non-Functional RequirementsEase of Use: Minimalistic UI focused on a "Microphone" button.Accuracy: High-precision Speech-to-Text (STT) to capture technical jargon in local dialects.Latency: AI responses should be fast enough to maintain a natural conversational flow.
